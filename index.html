RESEARCH INTERESTS

My research interest is in the general areas of statistical signal processing, wireless communications, and machine learning, including but not limited to

  • Bayesian learning

  • Compressed sensing

  • Intelligent communications

  • Network information theory.

Students with relevant backgrounds and mathematical skills are welcome to apply for graduate, doctoral and postdoctoral programs.
