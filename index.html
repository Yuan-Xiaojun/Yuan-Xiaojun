RESEARCH INTERESTS

My research interest is in the general areas of statistical signal processing, communications, and information theory, including but not limited to

  • Network information theory

  • 5G wireless communications and beyond

  • Compressed sensing and structured signal recovery

  • Machine learning.

Students with a relevant subject background and a good mathematical foundation are welcome to apply for graduate programs, doctoral programs and post-doctoral programs.
